12/13-Right/Left Most Caudal Point on Medial Epicondyle, 
14/15-Right/Left Most Caudal Lateral Point on the Radial Styloid, 
16/17-Right/Left Most Caudal Medial Point on the Ulnar Styloid, 
18/19-Right/Left Anterior Superior Iliac Spine, 
20/21-Right/Left Posterior Superior Iliac Spine, 
22/23-Right/Left Femoral Epicondyle, 
24/25-Right/Left the Most Lateral Point on the Border of the Lateral Tibial Condyle,

28/29-Right/Left Tip of Medial Malleolus (on the shoes),
